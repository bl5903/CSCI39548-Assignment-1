The toco toucan is the most popular type of toucan. Toucans can be found in central and eastern South America

Toucans live inside holes in trees. They cover their beak by tucking it under their wings which allows them to blend in with the darkness

Toucans eat fruits such as pears,oranges,and guavas. They also eat insects, eggs, and young birds
